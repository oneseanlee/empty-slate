**After completing a lesson:**

1. **Click "Take Quiz"** button
2. **Read each question carefully**

4. **Review all answers** before submitting
5. **Click "Submit Quiz"**

### Understanding Results

**Immediate Feedback:**
- See your score (percentage)
- View which questions you got right/wrong
- Read explanations for correct answers
- Learn from mistakes

**Passing Score:** 70%

**If You Pass:**
- Earn bonus XP!
- Move to next lesson
- Build your knowledge base

**If You Don't Pass:**
- No worries! Retake unlimited times
- Review the lesson again
- Focus on areas you missed
- Try again when ready

**Quiz Tips:**
- Read questions thoroughly
- Eliminate obviously wrong answers
- Use lesson knowledge to reason through answers
- Don't guess randomly - understanding matters
- Learn from explanations even if you got it right

## Tips for Success

### Learning Strategies

**1. Create a Learning Schedule**
- Set aside dedicated study time
- Even 15-30 minutes daily makes a difference
- Consistency beats marathon sessions
- Use calendar reminders

**2. Start with Foundations**
- Begin with "Getting Started" category
- Build on fundamentals before advanced topics
- Don't skip ahead too quickly
- Mastery > speed

**3. Apply What You Learn**
- Pull your actual credit reports
- Identify errors using course knowledge
- Practice writing dispute letters
- Implement strategies in real life
- Learning by doing reinforces concepts

**4. Take Notes**
- Keep a notebook or digital doc
- Summarize key points in your own words
- Note questions to research further
- Create your personal action plan

**5. Retake Quizzes**
- First attempt: Test what you know
- Review explanations thoroughly
- Second attempt: Reinforce learning
- Aim for 100% to ensure mastery

**6. Build Your Streak**
- Log in daily, even briefly
- Complete at least one lesson per day
- Earn streak badges
- Momentum creates habit

**7. Join the Community** (future feature)
- Ask questions in forums
- Share your experiences
- Learn from others' situations
- Help fellow learners

### Credit Repair Journey Tips

**Be Patient:**
- Credit repair takes 3-6 months minimum
- Quick fixes don't exist (beware scams)
- Consistent effort produces results
- Celebrate small victories

**Stay Organized:**
- Use ScorePro to track your learning
- Keep credit report copies organized
- Document all disputes and responses
- Maintain a credit repair binder/folder

**Follow the Law:**
- Know your FCRA rights
- Dispute legitimate errors only
- Keep records of all communication
- Don't fall for illegal tactics

**Build While Repairing:**
- Establish positive credit habits now
- Open secured card if needed
- Become authorized user strategically
- Pay all bills on time going forward

### Platform Usage Tips

**Maximize XP:**
- Complete full courses (not just individual lessons)
- Pass all quizzes
- Maintain daily streak
- Earn all badges

**Track Progress:**
- Check dashboard weekly
- Review which categories need focus
- Set personal goals (e.g., "finish 2 courses this month")
- Celebrate milestones

**Use Filters:**
- Filter courses by category
- Search for specific topics
- Focus on your weak areas
- Revisit advanced courses after building foundation

## FAQ

### Account & Access

**Q: Is my data secure?**  
A: Yes. We use industry-standard encryption, secure authentication, and strict data protection policies. Your information is never shared with third parties.

**Q: Can I access on mobile?**  
A: Yes! The platform is fully responsive and works on phones, tablets, and computers.

**Q: Can I log in from multiple devices?**  
A: Yes, use the same email and password on any device.

**Q: What if I forget my password?**  
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the reset instructions sent to your email.

### Courses & Content

**Q: How many courses are there?**  
A: 87 courses across 12 categories, covering all aspects of credit repair and financial health.

**Q: How long are the courses?**  
A: Lessons average 10-15 minutes to read. Complete courses range from 1-3 hours total.

**Q: Are there videos?**  
A: Video integration is coming soon. Currently, courses are high-quality written content.

**Q: Can I download lessons?**  
A: Offline access is a planned feature. Currently, internet connection required.

**Q: Is content updated?**  
A: Yes, we regularly review and update content to reflect current laws and best practices.

### Quizzes & Progress

**Q: What happens if I fail a quiz?**  
A: Nothing bad! Retake it unlimited times. Use it as a learning opportunity.

**Q: Do I have to take quizzes?**  
A: No, quizzes are optional but highly recommended to reinforce learning and earn XP.

**Q: Can I retake a quiz for more XP?**  
A: No, XP is awarded only on first pass. But you can retake to improve understanding.

**Q: Is my progress saved automatically?**  
A: Yes, progress saves in real-time. Resume anytime from any device.

### Certificates

**Q: How do I get a certificate?**  
A: Complete all courses and quizzes in a category.

**Q: Are certificates recognized?**  
A: They demonstrate your knowledge of credit repair. While not official certifications, they show commitment and education.

**Q: Can I print certificates?**  
A: PDF download feature coming soon. Currently viewable online.

### Billing

**Q: Can I cancel anytime?**  
A: Yes, cancel anytime from your Billing page. Access continues until period ends.

**Q: Do you offer refunds?**  
A: Yes, within 30 days of purchase if unsatisfied. Contact support.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**

**Q: Is there a discount for annual payment?**  
A: Future feature. Currently monthly billing only.

**Q: What happens if my payment fails?**

### Technical Issues

**Q: The site won't load. What should I do?**  
A: Try: Refresh page, clear browser cache, try different browser, check internet connection.

**Q: I can't see my progress/XP.**  
A: Refresh the page. If issue persists, log out and back in.
